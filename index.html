ASCE EWRI Task Committee on ML/AI Applications in Water Engineering

The ML/AI Applications in Water Engineering Task Committee is dedicated to advancing the integration of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies within the realm of water engineering. Our primary objective is to facilitate the understanding and adoption of ML/AI tools among engineers working in various domains of water management. Through collaborative efforts, the committee endeavors to bridge the gap between ML/AI tool developers and practitioners in the water engineering sector.
